About

I am currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Dr. Chee Wei Tan and Dr. Chung Chan. I received the CityU Outstanding Academic Performance Award for my Ph.D. studies. I was a Machine Learning Center Research Intern at the Tencent AI Lab, mentored by Dr. Tan. We received the Honorable Mention Award at the MIT/Amazon/IEEE Graph Challenge 2018 for our collaborative research paper.

My research interests span big data analytics and artificial intelligence, with a focus on healthcare and education applications. Specific research projects include optimizing machine learning for pandemic responses, addressing misinformation in crises, and enhancing autonomous learning in education systems. More details about my research and publications can be found in my research statement and CV.
